Brief Intro

Namaste! I'm Ranjeet, a passionate full-stack web developer hailing from the serene landscapes of Unnao, Uttar Pradesh. My educational journey started in Gurugram, Haryana, where I completed my schooling up to the 12th standard. My journey led me to excel in web development through intensive training at Masai School. Proficient in both front-end and back-end technologies, I'm committed to crafting seamless, user-centric web solutions that marry functionality with aesthetics.
